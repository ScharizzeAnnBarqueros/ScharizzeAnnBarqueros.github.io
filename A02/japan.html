Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji, Japan's tallest mountain, is an active stratovolcano located on Honshu Island. It is a symbol of Japan and is known for its symmetrical cone, particularly when viewed from a distance.

Tokyo Tower

Tokyo Tower is a communications and observation tower in the district of Shiba-koen in Minato, Tokyo, Japan, completed in 1958. At 332.9 meters, it was the tallest tower in Japan until the construction of Tokyo Skytree in 2012.

Osaka Castle

Osaka Castle is a Japanese castle in Chūō-ku, Osaka. The castle is one of Japan's most famous landmarks and played a major role in the unification of Japan during the sixteenth century.

Universal Studios Japan
