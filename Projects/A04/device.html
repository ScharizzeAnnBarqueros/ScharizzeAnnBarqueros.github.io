Infinix Hot 30

The Infinix Hot 30 offers a 6.78-inch FHD+ display with a 90Hz refresh rate, powered by the MediaTek Helio G88 for smooth performance. It features a 50MP AI camera, 5000mAh battery with 33W fast charging, and DTS audio for an immersive experience, making it a great choice for everyday use.

Specifications

  • Display: 6.78-inch FHD+ (1080 x 2460), 90Hz refresh rate
  • Processor: MediaTek Helio G88
  • RAM: 8GB (with up to 8GB virtual RAM extension)
  • Storage: 128GB (expandable via microSD)
  • Rear Camera: 50MP AI dual-camera system
  • Front Camera: 8MP
  • Battery: 5000mAh with 33W fast charging
  • Operating System: XOS 12.6 based on Android 13
  • Connectivity: 4G LTE, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth
  • Audio: DTS sound technology
  • Security: Side-mounted fingerprint sensor, Face unlock
  • Other Features: Dual SIM, USB Type-C, 3.5mm headphone jack
