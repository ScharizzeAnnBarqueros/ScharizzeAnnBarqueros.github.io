Weirdmageddon Survival Guide

When the world is falling apart, and the weirdness takes over, follow these essential rules to survive the madness:

Rule #1: Trust No One... Especially Triangles

If you see a floating triangle with a top hat and a creepy smile, RUN.

Rule #2: Stay Away from the Rifts

Weirdness rifts are portals to other dimensions. You don't want to fall into one.

If you see glowing cracks in the sky, move in the opposite direction!

Rule #3: Hide Your Mind

Cover your head with a tin foil hat—it’s cliché, but it works!

The weirdness can mess with your thoughts. Protect your brain at all costs.

Rule #4: Befriend the Weird

Some creatures are friendly (or at least, less dangerous than others).

Befriending the right ones might save your life. Bribe them with snacks, riddles, or a really good joke.

Rule #5: Avoid Suspicious Deals

No matter how desperate you are, don’t shake hands with anything glowing, floating, or offering “great deals.”

Rule #6: Find Shelter

Seek refuge in places shielded from weird energy, like underground bunkers or tree forts.

Bring plenty of snacks. The apocalypse is more tolerable with chips.

Rule #7: Decode the Weirdness

Weirdmageddon comes with cryptic symbols and riddles—solve them to find hidden truths or escape routes.

Look for patterns in the stars, trees, or even cereal boxes.

Rule #8: Never Give Up

The weirdness thrives on chaos, but staying hopeful and brave can help you outlast the madness.

Remember, all realities have rules—figure them out and bend them to your advantage.
